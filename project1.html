School management system

  • Live Demo

I built a school management system for St. Therese vocational training centre to keep record of the students and teachers in the institution. The system is built with Next.js for the frontend and Express.js (Node.js) for the backend and it uses MySQL as the primary database

Some of the features of the system include :

  • Storage of student information with full CRUD functionality
  • Tracking of fees information for all students depending on their course
